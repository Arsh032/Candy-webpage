Cocoa Life – 100% sustainably sourced cocoa. We source our cocoa from a number of countries, and in each of these countries Cocoa Life is at the heart of what we do there. It all began in 2012 with the ambition to help communities in cocoa regions. From farming techniques, to looking after the environment and supporting the local economy, it supports the community as a whole. Today we’re proud to say that Cadbury Dairy Milk cocoa is sustainably sourced through Cocoa Life, and by 2025 we’re aiming to say the same for all Cadbury products.
